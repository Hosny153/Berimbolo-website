Standard security package

For the discerning customer seeking a robust yet accessible security solution, our "Standard Security Package" delivers on both fronts. This comprehensive package offers an array of features designed to fortify your property's defenses, all while maintaining affordability and user-friendliness.

Alarm System

At the heart of our Standard Security Package lies a state-of-the-art alarm system. Engineered to detect and deter potential threats, this system provides an immediate response to unauthorized access or security breaches. With intuitive controls and customizable settings, you can easily manage and optimize your security parameters to suit your specific requirements.

CCTV Camera

Enhancing your surveillance capabilities, this package includes not one, but two high-definition CCTV cameras. Strategically positioned to provide maximum coverage, these cameras capture crisp, clear footage of your surroundings. Whether monitoring indoor spaces, outdoor perimeters, or critical entry points, our CCTV cameras deliver unparalleled visibility and peace of mind.

Local CCTV Footage Storage

Recognizing the importance of preserving security footage, our Standard Security Package offers both online and local storage options. With online storage, you can securely store and access your CCTV footage via the internet, allowing for convenient remote viewing and playback. Additionally, local storage ensures that video recordings are safely archived within your premises, providing redundancy and reliability in case of connectivity issues or external threats.

NOT Centrally Monitored

Unlike traditional security systems that rely on centralized monitoring services, our Standard Security Package operates independently. This means you retain full control over your security infrastructure without the ongoing cost or reliance on external monitoring entities. While centralized monitoring offers certain advantages, such as rapid response to alarms, our package provides a cost-effective alternative for those seeking self-managed security solutions.
